Part 3 - Story - Activity

  • You will have a title or a sentence.
  • You must write a story that follows the title or the sentence
  • You should respect the style of the sentence, for example, if it is in 3rd person, you must write in 3rd person all the text.

Part 3 - Story - Tips

  1. Write stories by yourself
  2. Read english simplified stories for understand structure.
