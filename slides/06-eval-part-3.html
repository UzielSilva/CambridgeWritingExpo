Part 3 - Activity

  • You should choose either writing a informal letter, or a story
  • The text must have around 100 words

Part 3 - Tips

  1. Make a draft before writing, it helps you with organization of ideas
  2. You should be aware of the number of the words, it indicates insufficient, or irrelevant information
  3. Practice with your classmates writing letters or stories and watching what information they are including
  4. Choose the task which best suits you and your interests

Part 3 - Assessment

  • You will be evaluated in 4 categories: Content, Communicative Achievement, Organization and Language
  • You should use a range of tenses, expressions and vocabulary, even if you make some minor mistakes.
  • Minor errors that don't affect communication will not necessarily be penalized
