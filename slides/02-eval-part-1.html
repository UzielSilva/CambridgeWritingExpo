Part 1 - Activity

  • Five sentences about the same topic
  • Each question has one complete sentence, followed by a gapped sentence below
  • You need to fill the gap with maximum 3 words so the two sentences should have the same meaning

Part 1 - Tips

  1. Read the complete sentence
  2. Read the second sentence and find what words was changed
  3. Think about which phrases you could use to complete it
  4. Complete the sentence with a maximum of 3 words
  5. Verify both sentences have the same meaning

Part 1 - Assessment

  • If you use more than 3 words you will lose the mark
  • Spelling is important
  • The question could have one or more possibly answers

Part 1 - Assessment

  • Evaluation based on the Language Specifications(Page 66 of Cambridge English Preliminary Booklet)
  • Studying past exams is a good approach for identifying areas that are typically tested. Ex: prepositions, collocations, passive/active voice, direct and indirect speech, verb patterns and opposites.
